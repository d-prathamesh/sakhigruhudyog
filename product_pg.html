1. Fasting Mix



FASTING MIX

Ingredients :-
● Amaranth (राजगिरा)
● Sago (साबुदाना)
● Bhagar (भगर)


Procedure:-

1) Mix 2 small boiled Potatoes (smashed), 1 tablespoon grinded peanuts, green chilli or Red chilli powder (as per taste) to the "SAKHI" Fasting
Bhajani Flour in a Mixing Bowl. 2) Add Water or ButterMilk (as per your requirement) and mix it with the Flour. Knead the dough for 5 minutes.
3) Take a small piece of the Dough, make a ball out of it and roll it with the rolling pin until evenly circular shaped.
4) Preheat the Pan with a Ghee layer and place our Dough and late it bake.
5) Bake it till the Dough turns out a bit Golden Yellowish in color.
6) Serve the "ThalPith" with Butter or Curd and Enjoy it.

You can also prepare Dhapate, Bhakri, shreera using SAKHI'S Fasting mix along with Thalpith.

2. Thalpith Bhajni



THALPEETH BHAJNI

Ingredients -
● Sorghum
● Wheat
● Gram dal
● Green dal
● Urad dal
● Coriander
● Cumin
● Ova


Procedure:-

1) While making thalapeeth, add finely chopped onion, red chilli powder, salt and cilantro to taste in the SAKHI'S Thalpeeth Bhajnee.
2) Add water or buttermilk as required, mix well and soak for five minutes.
3) Take a small dough and place it on the pan.
4) Preheat the Pan with a Ghee layer and place our Dough and late it bake.
5) Enjoy a hot plate with pickles, chutney or curd.

3. Chakli Bhajni



CHAKLEE BHAJNEE

Ingredients:
● Rice
● Gram Dal
● Urad Dal
● Green Dal
● Coriander
● Cumin
● Ova


Procedure:-

1) Add chilli powder, salt, turmeric powder, sesame seeds and oil to the SAKHI'S chakli bhajani.
2) Mix it well. Soak or boil in lukewarm water.
3) Prepare Chaklis with the help of Chakli Maker.
4) Fry in hot oil and enjoy a hot, crispy khamang chakli.
