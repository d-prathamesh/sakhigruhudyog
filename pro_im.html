INSTANT DHOKLA MIX

Ingredients :-

● Rice
● Gram Dal
● Urad Dal
● Salt
● Sugar
● Baking Soda
● Nimbu Satva( Citric Acid )


Procedure:-

1) Mix one cup of water with SAKHI'S one cup of dhokla mix.
2) If steaming in microwave oven, keep for 5 minutes. If steaming in the pressure cooker, keep for 15 minutes.
3) After 5 minutes of steaming, Add a tadka with Mustard, cumin, asafoetida.

Enjoy spongy, teasty, delicious Khamang Dhokla.!



INSTANT IDLI MIX

Ingredients :-

● Rice
● Urad Dal


Procedure:-

1) Mix water in SAKHI'S idli mix as per your requirement.
2) Soak this mixture for at least five hours.
3) While making idli add salt and baking soda to taste in the mixture.
4) Steam it from idli Maker.
And your soft, fluffy, spongy Idli is ready!



INSTANT DAL- BATI MIX

Ingredients:

● Wheat
● Gram Dal
● Maize


Procedure :

1) Add salt, oil, curd and a little baking soda to taste in the SAKHI'S Dal-bati mix.
2) Mix the mixture and add water as required to make a thick dough.
3) Soak the dough for 10 minutes.
4) Take a round shaped piece f the dough and boil it in water.
5) Divide the dough into 8-10 small pieces each and fry it in hot oil.
6) Bake the Balls in Tandoor - Maker or can also make "Batti" by soaking them in Ghee.

Serve the Dal Batti with hot Dal and Enjoy it.
