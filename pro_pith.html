1. Satu Pith


BARLEY FLOUR (SATU PITH)

Ingredients :-

● Wheat
● Gram Dal
● Ginger
● Nutmeg
● Cardamom

Suitability :- Barley Flour is economical as well as Nutritious. It helps in building Immunity. It is beneficial especially for small kids and for the Aged ones. You must include Barley Flour in your Diet to build up Stamina.


Procedure:-

1) In day-to-day life, Barley Flour is served with Milk or Water mixed in it and added with Sugar or Jaggery to add taste and liquify as per the need.
2) Also you can make Laddu by adding Ghee, Suggar or Jaggery into Sakhi's Barley Flour.
3) You can also prepare Thalapeeth from this mixture.

2. Rajgira Pith


AMARNTH FLOUR / RAJGIRA PITH

Ingredients -

● Amaranth(राजगिरा)


Procedure:-

1) Add red chilli powder, salt, cumin in SAKHI'S rajgira flour as per your requirement and make its dough.
2) Take a small piece of dough and prepare puris from it.
3) Fry puris in hot peanut oil or ghee.

Uses :
You can also make Thalpeeth, Dhapate, sweet and spicy puris etc.

SAGO SEED'S FLOUR / SABUDANA PITH

● Sogo Seeds (Sabudana)

2) You can make laddus from this flour.

4. Bhagar Pith


VARAI's FLOUR / BHAGAR PITH

Ingredients:

● Bhagar ( Varai )


Procedure :

1) Add buttermilk in Sakhi's bhagar pith and make a Dosa mix like flour.
2) Add salt as per your requirement after 30 minutes.
3) Preheat the Pan with a Ghee layer and prepare fasting dosa from it.
You can also make bhakree from Sakhi's Bhagar flour.
